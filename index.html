Intro of Bakery

A bakery is a business that specializes in producing and selling baked goods like bread, cakes, pastries, and cookies. It can range from a small, artisan shop focused on high-quality, handcrafted products to a larger commercial operation supplying goods to stores and restaurants. Bakeries typically involve various stages of preparation, including mixing ingredients, baking, cooling, and decorating. They serve both retail customers directly through storefronts and online platforms, as well as wholesale clients.

                

Components of a Bakery Production Area

  • Mixing: Where ingredients are combined. This can include stand mixers for small batches or large industrial mixers for bigger operations.
  • Baking: Ovens of different types (convection, deck, etc.) are used to bake the items.
  • Cooling: Racks or cooling stations where baked goods are allowed to cool before packaging or selling.
  • Packaging: The area where baked goods are wrapped and prepared for sale.
